Facts About Bears

Read time: 2 minutes or depends on your English language knowledge

Bears are some of the mammals. Bears can be up to 500 kg in weight. Bears occupy many continents. Bears own sharp claws and sometimes complicated character.Bears as some poeple prefer different diets depending on the lifestyle and occupation, but survival is more possible for the rounded bear than the skinniest one.Bears prefer honey if people do not eat it first. Polar bears to keep their figures nice prefer keto diet time after time.Brown bears consider salmon as their first choice not only on the special days of the life.Even termites can not stop bears from eating fast as bears could visit insect restaurant in open air since childhood.Bears as some human nations may experience the habitat loss due to the different reasons and lack of solicitors representing them in the international courts.Bears do not favour changes of climate due to the desire to avoid unnecessary changes of the outfits and diet and lack of finances to move somewhere else.Lets save the bears as the unique creatures of Nature whose existance is absolutely vital.
